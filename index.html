Biography

I am a PhD student in the Department of Political Science at the University of Notre Dame and a PhD Fellow at the Kellogg Institute for International Studies. My work has focused on conflict and violence, democratic and authoritarian institutions, and local governance, with a regional focus on Latin America. My methodological interests center on causal inference, experimental designs, and machine learning. My work has been published in Legislative Studies Quarterly (LSQ).
